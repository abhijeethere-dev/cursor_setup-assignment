**Raw post text:**

Every piece of lazy AI content says the same thing to your reader: your time is worth less than mine.

Low-quality content used to be rare because it was expensive to produce. Now it feels free. 20 bucks a month. Unlimited LinkedIn posts. Endless blogs. So the floor completely fell out.

The disease is entitlement to someone else's attention. The default output of every AI tool is: sound smart, say nothing, respect nobody's time.

If you're using AI to write, great. If you're using AI to avoid writing, your audience already knows.

PS: I even used AI to write this post — but the right way. A Claude skill integrated with Fathom's MCP pulls all my calls from the last 7 days, analyzes them, and reminds me of the smart stuff I said. I'm using AI to create better. Not to care less.
