**Raw post text:**

I found myself sharing a basket of fries over the weekend and thought... what would life be like if you were a french fry? So I built a game this weekend where you play a french fry trying not to get eaten by a giant human.

Every week my plan is to ship something to learn how the future of the internet/tech works — because everything is changing so fast with AI.

Here's what I learned:
- Game libraries run smoothly on Cloudflare Pages, 3D models are more difficult than 2D sprites
- We will see the rise of web "experiences" as Agents consume the information discovery layer. If you were around during StumbleUpon days, web experiences must offer something to capture real users
- AI models are still at ground 0-1 for serious game development
