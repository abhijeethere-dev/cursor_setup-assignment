**Topic:** Agency Growth & Delivery Capacity

---

**Key Insight:** Growth formula for SEO agencies:

> Growth = (Leads × Conversion Rate) × Delivery Capacity

Most agencies obsess over leads and conversion but ignore delivery capacity — the bottleneck that keeps them stuck even when sales improve.

**Takeaway:** Doubling delivery capacity is the fastest lever for agency growth. Relevant to AI SEO because AI-powered content production is one of the primary ways agencies scale delivery capacity without proportionally scaling headcount.

---

**Raw post text:**

Growth = (Leads × Conversion Rate) × Delivery Capacity

A lot of agencies focus on the first two. Ignore the third… and you stay stuck.

If you can't handle more work, you can't grow - even if you sell more.

Double your delivery capacity, and growth gets a whole lot easier.
