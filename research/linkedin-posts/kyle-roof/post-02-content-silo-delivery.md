**Raw post text:**

Not all SEO content is the same - and neither is the way we deliver it.

Inside POP White Glove, every client gets a content silo made of:
- Top Level Content → The "parent" page for your main keyword (service, location, product)
- Supporting Content → Articles that strengthen the Top Level page's authority and rankings

Then, you choose how you want that content delivered:

1. Content Brief / Outline — marked-up page outline, schema, and instructions for your writers
2. Existing Page Update Brief — detailed brief to re-optimize published pages, includes schema
3. AI-Generated Draft — full draft fast, using AI + POP optimization, your team handles final proofing
4. Human-Edited Content — written and fact-checked, optimized for structure, accuracy, and SEO
