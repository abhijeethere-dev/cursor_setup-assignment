**Topic:** B2B GEO Study — Versus Content (X vs Y) Drives 2x More AI Search Traffic

---

**Key Insight:** A study of 116 B2B sites found that "X vs Y" comparison pages drive double the AI search traffic of any other content type — beating "best X software" and "X alternatives" pages.

**Study methodology:**
- 116 B2B sites, 1,112 transactional pages
- GA4 data correlated against AI search referrals from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, and Meta AI over 90 days

**Key findings:**
- Versus pages had a Spearman correlation of 0.65 — roughly 2x the next-best content type
- Why: LLMs cite pages that match "X vs Y" intent exactly; best-of lists compete with G2/Capterra for the same citation slot
- **First 20 transactional comparison pages = biggest traffic curve bend** — going from 1-5 to 6-20 pages = 350% median lift in AI search sessions; 21+ adds another 250%
- Content explains only 28% of variance in B2B AI search traffic — 72% is brand pull and category fit
- 1 in 5 top-traffic sites had fewer than 5 transactional pages — brand alone can get you referenced

**Strategic takeaway for AI SEO content production:** If you're using AI to produce comparison content at scale, prioritize "X vs Y" format over "best X" lists. The first 20 versus pages produce disproportionate returns.
