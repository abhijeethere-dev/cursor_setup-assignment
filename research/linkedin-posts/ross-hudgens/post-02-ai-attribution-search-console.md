**Topic:** AI Attribution in Search Console is Incomplete — Unlinked Mentions Still Untracked

---

**Key Insight:** Google's new Search Console AI impression data only tracks URLs that appear in AI features — NOT unlinked brand mentions, which are currently the most valuable and most common form of AI visibility.

**The problem:**
- Google is adding impression data for URLs appearing in AI features
- But unlinked mentions in AI (e.g. "Ahrefs" or "Semrush" mentioned without a link) are NOT tracked
- These unlinked mentions are currently the most valuable form of GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) visibility
- Without this data, it's impossible to properly show the ROI of GEO programs to clients

**Example:** In a prompt result, Ahrefs and Semrush receive brand mentions with all the value — but neither would receive a tracked impression under the new system.

**Why this matters for AI SEO content production:** If you're producing AI-optimized content to get brand mentions in AI results, you currently have no reliable way to measure whether it's working. This is the #1 measurement gap in GEO right now.

**Raw post text:**

"AI attribution is solved with the new Search Console features." Not so fast.
