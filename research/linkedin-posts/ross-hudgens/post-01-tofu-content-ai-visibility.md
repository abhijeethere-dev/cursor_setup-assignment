**Raw post text:**

Big news: TOFU content is back!

If you search for product-led, truly top funnel topics, you will find company recommendations in AI results. The results come from companies that have created supporting top funnel content and are ranking for them — HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive for example.

The stress test our team is using: if it's possible to achieve at least 50% brand visibility on these prompts, it's a top funnel topic worth going after if the TOFU has significant search volume as a proxy of demand.

HubSpot achieved 83% visibility on "what is CRM" — that's not a top funnel topic wiping away our search universe, that's exactly the kind of influence we want from content marketing.

I could see re-indexing our search and topic universe as a pulse check to determine which keywords are actually returning brands. High visibility returns above 50% could indicate value beyond the click worth investing in.
