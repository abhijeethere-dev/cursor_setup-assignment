# AI-Powered SEO Content Production — Research Project

## What This Is
A structured research repository collecting high-signal content from 10 leading
practitioners on using AI to build and scale SEO content pipelines for B2B SaaS.

## Topic Chosen
**AI-powered SEO content production** — how modern teams use LLMs, automation,
and AI tooling to produce SEO content at scale without sacrificing quality or rankings.

## Why These Experts
Selected for direct practitioner experience (tool builders, agency operators,
in-house SEO leads) — not just writers or thought leaders. Priority given to people
who publish data, experiments, and real workflows.

## Repository Structure
- `/research/sources.md` — all 10 experts with links and annotations
- `/research/linkedin-posts/` — posts organized by author
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/` — transcripts organized by creator
- `/research/other/` — newsletters and podcast notes
